Section: title

  • A convenience for an <h1> with a title class applied to it. - The first paragraph of text is included in the h1

  • Following paragrpahs become regular <p> elements

Source
-> title

The clothes dried on a thin wooden rack

The stems of the tall glasses cracked and broke
Output
<h1 class="title">
  <p>The clothes dried on a thin wooden rack</p>
</h1>
<p>The stems of the tall glasses cracked and broke</p>

  • Attributes can be added via >> lines

Source
-> title
>> id: bravo

Fly by night, and you waste little time.

The chair looked strong but had no bottom.
Output
<h1 class="title" id="bravo">
  <p>Fly by night, and you waste little time.</p>
</h1>
<p>The chair looked strong but had no bottom.</p>

  • If a class attribute is added it is combined with the existing title class

Source
-> title
>> class: delta

We like to see clear weather

Tuck the sheet under the edge of the mat
Output
<h1 class="title delta">
  <p>We like to see clear weather</p>
</h1>
<p>Tuck the sheet under the edge of the mat</p>
