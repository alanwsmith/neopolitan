Section: pre

  • Preformatted content

  • Translates to the HTML <pre> element

  • The text is passed through the standard HTML escape process

  • No other alterations are made

  • Leading and trailing whitespace is trimmed so the text connects directly with the opening and closing pre tags

Source
-> pre

The kite flew wildly in 
the high wind.

The <strong>tin box</strong> held 
priceless stones.
Output
<pre>The kite flew wildly in 
the high wind.

The &lt;strong&gt;tin box&lt;&#x2f;strong&gt; held 
priceless stones.</pre>

  • Accepts key/value pairs on >> lines which become attribute names and values in the output

Source
-> pre
>> class: echo

Shut the hatch before the waves 
push it in.

Crack the walnut with your sharp 
side teeth.
Output
<pre class="echo">Shut the hatch before the waves 
push it in.

Crack the walnut with your sharp 
side teeth.</pre>
