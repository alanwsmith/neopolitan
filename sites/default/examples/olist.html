Section: olist

An ordered list

Source
-> olist

- The dry wax protects the 
deep scratch.

The marsh will freeze when 
cold enough.

- Twist the valve and release 
hot steam.
Output
<ol>
  <li>
    <p>The dry wax protects the deep scratch.</p>
    <p>The marsh will freeze when cold enough.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Twist the valve and release hot steam.</p>
  </li>
</ol>

Dashes are used instead of numbers to make it easier to move stuff up and down. (The numbering could be updated automatically on output, but it always bugs me when it's not accurate in the source file. Hence, dashes.)
