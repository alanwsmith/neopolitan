Section: notes

A list like section for notes. It's a <ul> with an explicit notes class applied as a convience approach.

Source
-> notes

- The music played on while they talked.

Dispense with a vest on a day like this.

-  The bunch of grapes was 
pressed into wine.
Output
<div class="notes">
  <ul>
    <li>
      <p>
        The music played on while they talked.
      </p>
      <p>
        Dispense with a vest on a day like this.
      </p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <p>
         The bunch of grapes was pressed into wine.
      </p>
    </li>
  </ul>
</div>
