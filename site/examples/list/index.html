Section: list

Basic unordered lists with the abbility to apply attributes.

Example
-> list

- There are more than two factors here.

The grass curled around the fence post.

- A wisp of cloud hung in the blue air.
Result
<ul>
  <li>
    <p>There are more than two factors here.</p>
    <p>The grass curled around the fence post.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>A wisp of cloud hung in the blue air.</p>
  </li>
</ul>
Example
-> list
>> id: sierra
>> class: oscar

- The salt breeze came across from the sea.

- Two blue fish swam in the tank.
Result
<ul id="sierra" class="oscar">
  <li>
    <p>The salt breeze came across from the sea.</p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Two blue fish swam in the tank.</p>
  </li>
</ul>
