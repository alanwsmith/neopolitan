Neopolitan

Neopolitan is a plain-text document format. It's designed to provide wide flexibility while maintaining a strong separation of concerns between content and its usage.

I've run my site for twenty years. In that time, I've undertaken a half dozen overhauls that required migrating content. As I think forward to the next twenty years, I never want to have to do that again. That's the goal of Neopolitan. To be the format for the next two decades.
