Section: p

  • Basic paragraphs

  • Each paragraph is separated by an empty line

  • Multiple connected lines are combined into a single <p> element

  • There's no section wrapper. Each paragraphs becomes it's own <p> element in the HTML output.

Source
-> p

Greet the new guests. A thin 
stripe runs down the middle.

Lush fern grow on the lofty rocks.
Output
<p>Greet the new guests. A thin stripe runs down the middle.</p>
<p>Lush fern grow on the lofty rocks.</p>

  • Attributes can be added with >> lines

  • Attributes are added to every <p> element in the output (if attributes need to be applied to a single paragraph put it in its own section)

Source
-> p
>> class: lima

A steep trail is painful for our feet.

Green moss grows on the northern side.
Output
<p class="lima">A steep trail is painful for our feet.</p>
<p class="lima">Green moss grows on the northern side.</p>
